SeTBAckS, 2011

Setbacks is the debut studio album made by ScHoolboy Q on his label Top Dawg Ent. This album was meant to display the struggles Q had while making this album, hence the name Stebacks. This album put Q on the map and begin his rap career.

Born in Wiesbaden, Hesse, West Germany, ScHoolboy Q has had a succesful career ScHooboy Q is signed to Top Dawg Ent. along with labelmates Kendrick Lamar, and Jay ROck. Q was also good friends with Mac Miller before his death by drugs in 2018.

OXYMORON, 2014

Oxymoron has been ScHoolboy Q's 2nd and has to be his most successful album to this date, with some supporting singles like "Collard Greens ft. Kendrick Lamar", "Studio ft. BJ the Chicago Kid", and "Man of the Year" to name a few.

HABITS & CONTRADICTIONS, 2012

Habits and Contradictions helped ScHoolboy Q rise even more in his rap career along with his labelmates at Top Dawg, like most notably Kendrick Lamar. This album had Single "Hands on the Wheel ft. A$AP Rocky and though not a single, I find the song "Blessed ft. Kendrick Lamar" to be very good.

BLANK FACE LP, 2016

ScHoolboy Q's most recent album, though it has been a while since it released. The album surprisingly doesn't feature any labelmates, but does have Kanye West on the single "That PArt" and E-40 on the song "Dope Dealer", and lastly Miguel and Justine Skye on the song "Overtime"
